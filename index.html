Tutorial Here.

1. Choose the content you want to annotate

When you select some content there will pop up a hook and a cross.

next »

2. Support it or not (Click hook or cross)

If you click the hook, which means you support the highlight content, the hook icon will become grey.

next »

3. After choosing one, you will get notification

After click hook or cross, click some other place and there will be a "Thank you" notification for you.

next »

4. You can see what people have annotated (include yourself)

When you put your mouse on the annotation that has been annotated, you can see others' opinion(Here you can see 1 support for the annotation).

next »

5. You can update your choice if you want

If you change your opinon from supporting to disagreeing, you can put your mouse on the annotation and click the other icon.

next »

6. After updating your choice, you will get a notification

After you click the cross, click some other place and then you will get the "Update" notification.
